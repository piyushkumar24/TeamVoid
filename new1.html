THE THREAT OF POACHING

Illegal poaching poses a significant threat to wildlife conservation efforts. Poachers often target endangered species, such as rhinos, elephants, and tigers, for their valuable parts like ivory, horns, or skins. Detecting and preventing poaching incidents is crucial for protecting these vulnerable species.

POACHING DETECTION SYSTEM

The objective of this project is to develop an AI-based system that can detect and alert authorities about potential wildlife poaching incidents in real-time. The system will integrate various technologies and data sources to identify suspicious activities and provide timely intervention to prevent poaching.

OUR PURPOSE & VISION

Our technology, a ground-breaking solution in the field of poaching prevention, provides quick detection, targeted interventions, and improved protection for endangered animals by utilizing real-time data processing and intelligent surveillance. We envision a world where poaching is effectively prevented, endangered species are protected, and ecosystems thrive. Through a global network of interconnected systems, community engagement, and collaboration, we aim to dismantle the illegal wildlife trade and create a future where wildlife populations flourish.

SIDE GOALS

Regarding the economic side of our proposed model, we would provide live footage of the wildlife areas on our website, which could be based on a subscription model. It can help tourist guides to take people to the relevant areas where wildlife will be abundant. In this way funds can also be raised from tourists. Injured animals can also be detected using this model.
